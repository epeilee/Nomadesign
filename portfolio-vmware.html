Form Management

The form system included three roles, the end-user, the form administrator, and the form auditor. The three roles have different page layouts for different permission levels. This is a very large and complex project, with the different user habits at every level taken into consideration. 表單系統涉及三個角色，用戶、表單管理員和表單審核員。三個角色針對不同的權限有不同的頁面佈局。這是一個非常龐大和復雜的項目，需要考慮到各個層面用戶的不同習慣。

Finance

The financial system consists of three parts, spending trends, the spending variance, and the cost center budget allocation. It can create monthly and departmental financial reports, compile the budget allocation for each month, list the months with overspending observed, and make cost breakdown inquiries. 財務系統包含三部分,一個是消費趨勢,一個是消費差異,最後是各部門花費. 可以完整的呈現每個月,每個部門的財報,甚至可以輸入每個月的預算分配,並且列出超支的月份,可以提出費用明細查詢.

Approval Process

There are four roles involved in the form approval process, the requester, the observer, the approver, and the back office administrator. When the requester sends out the request, the administrator will set 2-3 checkpoints depending on the level of the requester. Each delegate will be notified by email with an approval or rejection letter. Three different interfaces are required for the roles: the requester, the reviewer, and the backend manager, with all aspects of user behavior and the details of each role taken into account. 表單簽核流程有四個角色,需求者,觀察者,審核者,後台管理者.
當需求者送出需求後,管理者會依需求者職等而設定需要經過2-3個審核關卡審核,每一關卡會設定2-3審核者,只要其中一個審核者簽核或否決,就會往下一個關卡送審或是退回需求者.也會同時寄已簽核或已否決的信件通知每個角色.
需要設計需求者,審核者,後台管理者三種不同角色的介面,也能在設計時全方面思考不同角色的使用者行為,及需要注意的細節
