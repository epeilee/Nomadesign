About this Project

This flu testing app is designed for Medical Professionals who want to perform a large number of tests.
Step 1: Use the phone’s internal camera to take a picture of the test subject’s test strip.
Step 2: After 3 seconds of analysis of flu results
Step 3: The test results will show that Influenza A and B are positive or negative, and will tell you what symptoms to expect.
Step 4: Save the test results and data in the database, which is convenient for professionals to do mass screening and documentation.
