About this Project

This flu-testing app is designed for medical professionalsto perform and record a large number of tests, with the ability to upload or sync with patient database if required.
Step 1: Take a picture of the testing strip with the internal camera in the phone.
Step 2: Wait for3 seconds of analysis of flu results
Step 3: The test results will either be shown aspositive or negativefor the Influenza A and/or B viruses, and will tell you what the expected/ common symptoms are.
Step 4: Save the test results and the data will be uploaded to the database. Reduce the testing process and upload the test results manually, and allows for mass screening and documentation.
 這個流感測試App是為執行大量測試的專業醫療人員而設計的。
步驟1：使用手機的內置鏡頭拍攝測試試紙
步驟2：3秒鐘分析流感結果
步驟3：測試結果將顯示A型和B型流感陽性或陰性，並顯示會出現的症狀
步驟4：將測試結果和數據儲存在資料庫中，方便專業人員進行大規模篩选和記錄
